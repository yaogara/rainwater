    Rainwater Harvesting FAQ

    Is rainwater harvesting legal in my state?

    Rainwater collection is legal across the United States, though some states require permits or follow local plumbing code guidelines. Check your state page for specifics on legality and incentives.

    How do I know if an installer is certified?

    Look for contractors who are ARCSA Accredited Professionals or hold manufacturer certifications. Rainwater Directory highlights verified installers who meet industry standards.

    What incentives are available for rainwater systems?

    Incentives range from rain barrel rebates to tax exemptions and utility grants. Visit each state page to learn about current programs and links to apply.

    Can I use harvested rainwater indoors?

    Many states allow indoor non-potable uses such as toilet flushing or laundry when filtration and disinfection meet code. Always verify local requirements before connecting to indoor plumbing.
