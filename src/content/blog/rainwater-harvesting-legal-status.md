---
title: "Is Rainwater Harvesting Legal in the United States?"
description: "A state-by-state overview of regulations governing residential rainwater collection."
pubDate: 2024-05-12
author: "Rainwater Directory Team"
---

Collecting rainwater is legal in every U.S. state, but local rules can affect how systems are designed and used. States like Texas and Colorado have detailed plumbing codes that cover system design, while others simply recommend best practices. Always confirm whether permits are required for storage tanks, whether potable uses are allowed, and how stormwater fees or rebates apply. By understanding the regulations in your state, you can design a system that meets code and qualifies for available incentives.
